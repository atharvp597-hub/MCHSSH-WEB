Academic journey

From early years to senior secondary, every student is prepared for what comes next

Maharishi Convent Higher Secondary School follows the MP Board curriculum while bringing modern teaching practices to every classroom.

01

A nurturing foundation where young learners develop curiosity, discipline, and essential habits.

02

Senior secondary

Arts
