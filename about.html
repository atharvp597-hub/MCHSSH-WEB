About the School

Shaping confident learners with purpose and care

Founded in 2002, Maharishi Convent Higher Secondary School, Harda is a private higher secondary school dedicated to academic excellence, moral values, and modern learning for Nursery through Class 12.

Our vision and mission

Our vision is to provide quality education that develops knowledgeable, disciplined, confident, responsible, and compassionate students prepared for higher education and life.

Our mission is to deliver quality education with individual attention, build strong moral values, encourage academic excellence, promote creativity, and prepare responsible citizens.
