Principal's Message

Growing mindful, confident, and responsible learners.

At Maharishi Convent Higher Secondary School, Harda, we believe education is strongest when it combines knowledge, character, discipline, and compassion.

Our promise to families

We are committed to providing individual attention to each student, building strong moral values, and nurturing leadership qualities through a safe and modern learning environment.

Every child at MCHSSH is encouraged to explore, learn, and develop confidence through smart classrooms, active student activities, and a disciplined school culture.

Knowledge

Character

Values-based learning that turns knowledge into responsible action.

Opportunities in sports, culture, and school activities develop leadership skills.
